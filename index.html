How it works

Autofox is your trusted auto shop - with licensed mobile mechanics that come to you to work on your car. We can complete the vast majority of car repairs and maintenance remotely without the need of a garage or lift.

We help you save time and money. No more taking time out of your day to take your car to the garage. No more leaving it overnight. No more worries of being overcharged.

We’re committed to providing you the highest quality service and ensuring you receive a convenient and affordable service.

Contact us through our +1 234 567 8901 number or our form below. Let us know what kind of repair or maintenance you are looking for.If you don’t know what’s wrong with your car, we can book a FREE inspection for you.
We’ll provide you a quote that includes parts and labor for the service you need. Expect to save 20-30% compared to other garages offering the same service.
We’ll schedule a convenient time and place for one of our licensed mechanics to come and work on your car. We come toyou, with the parts your car needs, and complete the job.

About us

Co-founders message

We believe taking care of your car should be easy. At the moment, it’s an unpleasant hassle.

We know how much you depend on your car for work and life, how inconvenient it is dealing with most garages, and how unexpected car costs can really hurt your wallet. Add in the harsh winters and ongoing potholes of Canadian life, you and your car face even more stress.

That’s why, we’re on a mission to change how auto services work. We want to change your auto service experience to be more seamless, affordable and trustworthy.

Our team of auto technicians are fully licensed and have decades of experience working on both foreign and domestic makes & models. Not only will we provide a quality auto technician to make sure your car is running well, but we’ll come to you and it will cost you 20-30% less (on average).

We’re excited about serving the greater Toronto area and offering a new and better auto service experience for you.

If you’re interested in booking an appointment or just have a few questions, feel free to call us.

Looking forward to hearing from you!

Eden Dhaliwal,
Co-founder
