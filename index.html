Our company

We believe that all homes are beautiful. Unfortunately, we know they don't stay beautiful on their own, and there's a neverending stream of work required in order to keep them that way.

We also know how incredibly stressful and time-consuming it can be to find, evaluate, and hire a contractor you trust and have confidence in to do that work... let alone the work you'll need done in the future.

We have over 120,000 contractors on the JOIST platform worldwide, including an extremely deep network in North America's largest regional markets. Not only can we provide you with the perfect contractor for the work you need done now, we've also got you covered for future work as well... no matter what you need done. We're a one-stop shop for all of your home improvement needs!

Not only can JOIST Home provide you with the right Contractor for your needs, but we play an active role throughout the project in order to ensure:

  • Expectations are properly set so that the Contractor can get the job done just the way you want;
  • All communication lines are streamlined/efficient;
  • Constant updates to keep you informed on project progress;
  • Any concerns you have are addressed, by us, swiftly and to your satisfaction;
  • You barely have to lift a finger throughout the entire process, and are overjoyed with the final result

If you're interested in a free estimate appointment, or simply learning more about JOIST Home, just give us a call.

We look forward to speaking with you!
